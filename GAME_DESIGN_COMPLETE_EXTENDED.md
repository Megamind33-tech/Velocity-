## 6. RETENTION MECHANICS (Like Candy Crush)

### 6.1 DAILY/WEEKLY ROTATION
```
DAILY:
- 1 new Daily Challenge (changes every 24 hours)
- Specific song + specific target = 100 bonus XP
- Same challenge for ALL players (community event)
- Can replay unlimited times

WEEKLY BOSS:
- One song becomes the "weekly boss"
- Beat it on highest available level = special badge
- Leaderboard for weekly boss (top 100)
- Difficulty increases weekly

MONTHLY TOURNAMENT:
- All players compete on same set of 10 songs
- Leaderboard rankings determine rewards
- Top 100 get exclusive badges
- Resets monthly
```

### 6.2 REWARD SYSTEMS
```
PER LEVEL:
- Base XP: 50-1000 (depending on difficulty)
- Star rewards: 1 star = 50 XP, 2 stars = 100 XP, 3 stars = 150 XP
- Streak bonus: 1.1x - 2x multiplier for consecutive 3-stars
- Daily multiplier: 1.1x - 2.5x for daily play streaks
- Achievement unlocks: Special badges every 25 levels

CUMULATIVE:
- Every 100 levels beaten = level up + new cosmetic
- Every 500 XP earned = rank increase
- Every 10 3-stars = special badge
- Leaderboard milestones = exclusive rewards
```
